Meatloaf

Description

Meatloaf is an American staple. Quick and easy to make and thus the perfect weeknight dinner. Just grab that ground beef, throw in your seasoning, and pop that bad boy in the oven.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Onion
  • Barbeque Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Mix Ground Beef, Bread Crumbs, Onion together in a large bowl. Season to taste.
  3. Grease a loaf pan.
  4. Add beef mixture to pan.
  5. Bake for 40 minutes or until cooked through
  6. Add Barbeque Sauce and cook for 5 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
