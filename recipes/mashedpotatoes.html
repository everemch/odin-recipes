Mashed Potatoes

Description

Ever buy that big bag of potatoes and never use them? Yeah lets use those bad boys before they sprout and multiply.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Ranch Seasoning
  • Sour Cream
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Peel Potatoes and place in a bowl of cold water.
  3. Transfer peeled potatoes to a pot of boiling water.
  4. Once softened and able to be pierced easily, drain water.
  5. In a mixing bowl, add potatoes with all other ingredients.
  6. Mix with a hand mixer until smooth.
  7. Enjoy!
